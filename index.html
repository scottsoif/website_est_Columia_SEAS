TAME-AD Joint Cohort Explorer

is a tool for Alzheimer researchers

Python | Django | neo4j

  • The platform contains over 50,000 data records from multiple Alzheimer Disease studies.
    • These studies include EFIGA, WHICAP, PhenoGenetic (REDCap).
    • More studies are in the process of being added to our db.
  • An interactive Dash Plotly Pivottable allows researchers to examine these studies in depth.

The above two patents are for my Vehicular Heatstroke Prevention System

(VHPS)

Tragically, there have been over 850 reported U.S. child vehicular heatstroke deaths since 1998. My invention can stop these deaths.

  • My invention, the VHPS, can detect the presense of a child:
    • If the temperature rises to a preset threshold, the VHPS starts the vehicle's engine and air conditioning unit preventing any heatstroke to the child.
    • An Android app I wrote automatically contacts emergency personnel, providing GPS coordinates, directions and real-time information.

Vasios N, Gross AJ, Soifer S, Overvelde JTB, Bertoldi K. Harnessing Viscous Flow to Simplify the Actuation of Fluidic Soft Robots.
Soft Robotics. 2019. Harvard University, Cambridge, Massachusetts.

View Publication

This publication is for research I performed at Harvard Univeristy.

  • Soft robots have a variety of innovative use applications:
    • Surgery
    • Rescure operations on uneven terrain
    • Rehabilitation
    • Biomimicry
  • A fluidic control system allows fluidic control of soft robots
  • Soft robots use multiple inputs to perform intricate actions, such as flexible gripping or manipulating another object. We developed a more efficient design utilizing one individual input to perform multiple intricate movements. Fewer robotic parts can provide the same complex information to the robot while minimizing weight and creating a simpler user interface.
