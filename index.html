CrowdIt

is an iPhone app inspired by COVID-19.

Swift | MySQL | PHP

  • See how crowded a business is in real-time by tapping on an map marker or entering a store name using the search feature.
    • To properly socially distance it's best to know when a store is least busy during times of the Coronavirus pandemic.
  • Runs in background and sends location data and the devices 'identifierForVendor' to MySQL database.
  • Utilizes Google Places API and Maps SDK for ios.

TAME-AD Joint Cohort Explorer

is a tool for Alzheimer researchers.

Python | Django | neo4j

  • The platform contains over 50,000 data records from multiple Alzheimer Disease studies.
    • These studies include EFIGA, WHICAP, PhenoGenetic (REDCap).
    • More studies are in the process of being added to our db.
  • An interactive Dash Plotly Pivottable allows researchers to examine these studies in depth.
    • Features include bar charts, scatter plots, pie charts, tables, aggregations and much more.
  • Graphs allow researchers to study family patterns in Alzheimers.
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) is accessable to authenticated users with a login.

Release to public early fall 2020

View Patent

The above two patents are for my Vehicular Heatstroke Prevention System (VHPS)

Tragically, there have been over 850 reported U.S. child vehicular heatstroke deaths since 1998.

  • My invention, the VHPS, can detect the presense of a child:
  • If the temperature rises to a preset threshold, the VHPS starts the vehicle's engine and air conditioning preventing any harm to the child.
  • An Android app I wrote automatically contacts emergency personnel, providing GPS coordinates, directions and real-time information.
